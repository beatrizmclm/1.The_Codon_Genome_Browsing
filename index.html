5.a. What is anticodon?
The anticodon is a sequence of three nucleotide base pairs in the tRNA that code for one
amino acid and binds to the complimentary mRNA during protein synthesis in the ribosome.
5.b. What are the roles of anticodons?
The anticodons bind to the complimentary mRNA and its role is to get the correct amino acid
to create a protein.
